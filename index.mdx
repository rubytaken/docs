## What is heyRISA?

heyRISA is an AI-powered cryptocurrency intelligence platform that connects you to real-time market data through specialized AI agents. Each agent is trained for a specific domain — from technical analysis and price prediction to wallet tracking and DeFi optimization.

You chat with agents in natural language. They respond with live data, charts, sentiment scores, and actionable insights pulled from 40+ integrated tools.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask a question">
    Type your question in natural language. For example: "What's the Fear & Greed Index right now?" or "Analyze my Solana wallet."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent processes with live tools">
    The selected agent calls real-time APIs — CoinGecko, DexScreener, Helius, Dune Analytics — to gather current data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get actionable insights">
    Receive formatted analysis with charts, tables, confidence scores, and follow-up suggestions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
