## What are agents?

Agents are specialized AI assistants, each focused on a specific area of crypto analysis. When you select an agent, it uses a tailored system prompt and has access to the right tools for its domain.

You can browse and select agents from the **Agents** page in the app, or let the default chat auto-route your question to the best agent.
