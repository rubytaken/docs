## Core capabilities

heyRISA provides five major categories of crypto intelligence, all accessible through natural language chat.

### Real-time market analysis

Agents pull live data from CoinGecko, DexScreener, and CryptoCompare to give you current market conditions.

- **Price tracking** — live prices with 24h changes, market cap, and volume
- **Fear & Greed Index** — real-time market psychology from Alternative.me
- **Trending tokens** — top movers on DexScreener across all chains
- **Market overview** — top gainers, losers, and sector rotation

### Technical analysis

Professional-grade indicators calculated from real price data.

| Indicator | What it measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| RSI (Relative Strength Index) | Overbought/oversold conditions |
| MACD | Trend direction and momentum |
| Bollinger Bands | Volatility and price breakout signals |
| SMA/EMA | Short and long-term trend direction |
| Support/Resistance | Price levels where direction is likely to change |

Agents combine these indicators to produce **buy, sell, or hold signals** with confidence scores (for example, "82% confidence on this buy signal").

### Sentiment analysis

Track market psychology across multiple data sources.

- **Social media sentiment** — analysis from X (Twitter), Reddit, and crypto communities
- **News sentiment scoring** — AI-powered impact analysis of headlines
- **Crowd psychology** — contrarian signals based on sentiment extremes
- **Fear & Greed trends** — historical context for current market mood

### Wallet & portfolio analytics

Connect your wallet to unlock personalized insights.

- **Multi-chain scanning** — balances across Solana, Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, and 25+ chains
- **DeFi position tracking** — active yield farming, liquidity pools, and staking
- **NFT collection analysis** — rarity scores, floor prices, and portfolio breakdown
- **Risk assessment** — diversification scoring and rebalancing suggestions
- **Transaction history** — behavioral pattern analysis from past trades

### DeFi & NFT intelligence

Deep analysis of DeFi protocols and NFT markets.

- **Yield farming optimization** — compare APYs across protocols like Jupiter, Orca, and Raydium
- **Liquidity pool analysis** — impermanent loss calculations and risk comparison
- **NFT floor tracking** — real-time monitoring across collections
- **DEX activity** — new token launches and pair analysis via DexScreener

## Data sources

heyRISA connects to multiple live data providers:

| Provider | Data type |
|----------|-----------|
| CoinGecko | Prices, market cap, volume, historical data |
| DexScreener | DEX pairs, trending tokens, new launches |
| Alternative.me | Fear & Greed Index |
| Helius | Solana transactions, NFTs, DeFi positions |
| Dune Analytics | On-chain metrics, whale tracking, protocol analytics |
| CryptoCompare | Real-time price feeds, historical charts |

<Note>All data is fetched in real-time. Agents never use cached or stale data for analysis.</Note>
