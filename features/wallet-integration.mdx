## How to connect your wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the wallet panel">
    Click the wallet icon in the chat interface or ask an agent: "Analyze my wallet."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your wallet provider">
    Choose from supported providers: Phantom, MetaMask, Solflare, and others.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the connection">
    Your wallet will prompt you to approve a read-only connection. heyRISA never requests transaction signing permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start analyzing">
    Once connected, agents automatically detect your chain and balances. Ask questions like:
    - "What's my portfolio worth?"
    - "Am I too heavy on one coin?"
    - "Check my DeFi positions on Solana"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>heyRISA uses read-only wallet connections. Your private keys are never shared or stored.</Warning>

## What agents can analyze

### Token balances
Complete breakdown of all tokens with USD values, 24h changes, and portfolio percentage.

### DeFi positions
Active positions in liquidity pools, yield farms, lending protocols, and staking. Includes APY tracking and impermanent loss estimates.

### NFT collections
Full NFT inventory with:
- Collection name and floor price
- Rarity scores for individual items
- Estimated portfolio value
- Mint history and provenance

### Transaction history
AI-powered analysis of your past transactions to identify:
- Trading patterns and win/loss ratios
- Average hold times
- Gas spending trends
- Most profitable trades

## Multi-wallet support

You can scan wallets by address without connecting them. Paste any wallet address into the chat:

```
Analyze this wallet: 7xKXtg2CW87d97TXJSDpbD5jBkheTqA83TZRuJosgAsU
```

Agents will return the same detailed analysis for any public wallet address.
