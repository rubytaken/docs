## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.heyrisa.com](https://app.heyrisa.com) and sign up with your email or social login.

    After signing in, you land on the chat interface with a selection of suggestion categories:

    - **Real-time market analysis** — live prices, trending tokens, market overview
    - **Portfolio analysis** — wallet scanning across chains
    - **Trading signals** — buy/sell recommendations with confidence scores
    - **News & events** — crypto headlines, token unlocks, sentiment
    - **NFT & DeFi** — yield farming, NFT evaluation, protocol comparison
    - **Strategy & optimization** — allocation advice, DCA plans, risk management
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click the wallet icon in the chat interface to connect your Solana, Ethereum, or multi-chain wallet.

    Once connected, agents can:
    - Scan your token balances and DeFi positions
    - Analyze your NFT collections with rarity scores
    - Suggest portfolio rebalancing based on risk tolerance
    - Track your transaction history for behavioral insights

    <Tip>Wallet connection is optional. You can use all market analysis and trading signal features without connecting a wallet.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Select an agent or use the default chat. Type your question or click a suggestion to begin.

    **Try these first queries:**

    ```
    What's the Fear & Greed Index right now?
    ```

    ```
    Top gainers and losers in the last 24h?
    ```

    ```
    Analyze my wallet, should I rebalance?
    ```

    ```
    BTC buy or sell signal right now?
    ```

    Agents automatically select the right tools for your question. Responses include formatted tables, sentiment badges, and follow-up questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choose an AI model

heyRISA supports multiple AI models. You can switch between them from the model selector in the chat header.

| Model | Provider | Best for |
|-------|----------|----------|
| GPT-4o Mini | Azure | General chat with tool calling |
| GPT-4.1 | Azure | Complex reasoning and deep analysis |
| Pixtral Large | Mistral | Reasoning, writing, and image analysis |
| Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite | Google | Fast responses, high-volume tasks |
| Devstral Small | Mistral | Code generation and debugging |

<Note>All models on the free tier have daily usage limits. See your remaining queries in the chat interface.</Note>
